# Phase 2 — Competitive Niche Analysis

## Competitor Landscape: 24-Hour Mobile Tire & Roadside in Houston

The niche is crowded but weak. Every single site we examined runs on a DIY builder
(Wix, GoDaddy, Hibu, Weebly), has zero social proof on page, and leads with a phone
number instead of a conversion funnel. No one is animating. No one is using real
photography at scale. No one is telling a story.

**The bar is extremely low. A cinematic, premium, scroll-animated site will be the
#1 visual brand in the niche by default.**

## Patterns of the "Top" (really, the least-bad) sites

1. **Phone number as god** — every site puts the phone above the fold and repeats it. Consumers in tire emergencies call, they don't fill forms.
2. **24/7 repetition** — "24 HOURS" appears 3–6× per homepage. It's the category's table-stakes claim.
3. **Service list walls** — big grids of "we do X, Y, Z." No hierarchy, no storytelling.
4. **Zero animation** — not a single competitor uses scroll, parallax, or cinematic motion.
5. **Zero social proof** — none of the 5 shows a single testimonial, review count, or star rating on the homepage.

## What separates the eventual winner (us)

1. **Real photography** — trucks working in actual Houston neighborhoods at night, real techs.
2. **Animated, cinematic scroll** — treat this like an Apple product page. Nothing in the niche is within two generations of this aesthetic.
3. **Hard social proof above the fold** — pull real Google reviews, show the star count, show response times.
4. **A live "Send Help Now" flow** — one-tap call + form that captures location + tire issue in 10 seconds.
5. **7-location Houston proximity map** — nobody else visualizes how close you are to a shop.
6. **Commercial + consumer split** — one homepage, two clearly labeled paths: "Flat right now?" vs "Run a fleet?"

## Recommended design direction (evidence-backed)

- **Colors:** Fire red (`#E10600`) + asphalt black (`#0B0B0F`) + amber/warning yellow (`#FFC300`) + warm concrete (`#EDE8E0`). Urgency-coded palette. Competitors use washed-out blues, grey, or no color system at all.
- **Typography:** Industrial condensed display (Anton / Barlow Condensed) paired with Inter body. Echoes auto-shop / highway signage.
- **Motion:** GSAP + ScrollTrigger. Hero rolls in a tire. Phone number parallaxes. Numbers count up. Location pins drop on map.
- **Imagery:** Night-shot photography (flat tires, techs with work lights, Houston highways). Placeholder-marked for the user to generate via the Image Generator skill.
