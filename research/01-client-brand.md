## 4. Tone of Voice
- **Direct** — zero fluff, imperative verbs.
- **Emphatic** — heavy caps, repetition on availability.
- **Blue collar** — trust signals are about showing up, not credentials.
- **Reactive** — speaks to customers in the middle of a problem (flat at 2am).

## 5. Design Reality of the Current Site
- Built on GoDaddy's drag-and-drop builder. Template look.
- No real visual identity, no typography hierarchy, no photography, no social proof pulled to the page.
- Weak mobile experience. No schema. No on-page SEO for local terms.
- Logo is a raster blob — no transparent SVG version.
- **Huge opportunity gap** — competitors aren't doing cinematic, animated sites in this niche, so going premium will dominate.

## 6. Differentiators to Lean Into
1. **True 24/7** — not "call after hours," actually open.
2. **Mobile service across all of greater Houston** — they come to you.
3. **7 physical locations** — closest shop is minutes away.
4. **Full stack** — tires + alignment + towing + roadside in one call.
5. **Heavy-duty + passenger** — rare combo (they do 18-wheelers).
