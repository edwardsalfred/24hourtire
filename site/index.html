06 — Questions we hear at 3 a.m.

Real answers, fast.

Are you really open 24 hours?+

Yes — every day, every night, every holiday. Every location has staff on the clock, and the mobile dispatch line is live around the clock. If the phone doesn't get picked up in three rings, we owe you a free flat repair. That's never happened.

How much does a mobile tire call cost?+

A standard daytime mobile flat repair starts at $65 plus parts. Overnight rates apply from 11pm–6am. Most full tire replacements — including the mobile fee — land between $120 and $220 depending on the tire. We quote on the call before we roll.

Do you carry 18-wheeler tires in stock?+

Yes. Steer, drive, and trailer tires from major commercial brands. Our North Freeway and MLK shops are the two biggest commercial hubs, and our mobile units carry matching inventory for roadside calls.

How fast can you actually get to me?+

Inside the Loop, our average response time is around 12 minutes. The Beltway-to-Beltway sweet spot is 15–25 minutes. Further out we quote honestly when you call — no bait-and-switch arrivals.

Do you take cards and mobile payment?+

Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Cash App, Zelle, and cash. Fleet accounts get net-15 terms after credit approval.

Can you tow me if the tire isn't fixable?+

Yes. Every mobile truck can swap or patch on-site, and if the damage needs a lift, we have flatbed tow trucks at multiple shops. One call covers both outcomes.
